1. Add your legal company name

Right now it says:

“the provider of the Lens Lingo app”

That’s vague.

I’d change it to something like:

“Lens Lingo is operated by Integrated Sales Solutions LLC.”

This makes Apple, Google, and regulators happier because there is an actual legal entity behind the app.

⸻

2. Add a physical mailing address (recommended)

You currently only have:

mailto:support@lenslingo.ai

Legally, email alone is often sufficient for a small app, but GDPR regulators generally prefer a mailing address.

Something like:

Integrated Sales Solutions LLC
Reno, Nevada, United States
mailto:support@lenslingo.ai

You don’t necessarily need your home address if you have a business mailing address.

⸻

3. Be more specific about OpenAI retention

You currently say:

We use OpenAI’s API services…

I’d add:

OpenAI may temporarily retain API data in accordance with its published policies and security practices.

This protects you if OpenAI changes retention details.

⸻

4. Add a response timeline for deletion requests

You currently say:

within a reasonable period

I’d replace with:

within 30 days of receiving a verified deletion request, unless a longer period is required by law.

Much cleaner.

⸻

5. Make sure the app actually matches the policy

This is the biggest thing.

Your policy claims:

Users can delete conversations
Users can delete saved vocabulary
Users can request deletion
ATT controls Meta tracking
Firebase Analytics is used
Firebase Crashlytics is used

All of those must actually be true.

Apple reviewers sometimes check.

⸻

6. Verify App Store Privacy Nutrition Labels

This section caught my eye:

Linked to the user?

You are linking data to a persistent device UUID.

Apple generally considers that “linked.”

That part looks correct.

Just make sure App Store Connect selections match exactly.

⸻

7. Terms of Service page

I see:

Terms of Service

in the footer.

Make sure that page exists and contains:

Subscription terms
Auto-renewal language
Refund information
Limitation of liability
Acceptable use
Contact information

Apple is more likely to complain about missing Terms than this privacy policy.

⸻

8. Support page

Your support page only really needs:

Contact Support

Email: mailto:support@lenslingo.ai

Typical response time: 1–3 business days

For assistance with subscriptions, technical issues, privacy requests, or account questions, contact us at mailto:support@lenslingo.ai.

That’s enough for App Store purposes.

⸻

One thing I would remove

This sentence:

Your continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.

GDPR regulators don’t love implied consent language.

I’d replace it with:

Your continued use of the App after changes become effective is subject to the updated Privacy Policy.
